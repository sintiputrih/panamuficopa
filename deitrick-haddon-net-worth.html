Deitrick Haddon Wiki Biography

Deitrick Vaughn Haddon was born on the 17th May 1973, in Detroit, Michigan, USA. He is engaged in various activities including acting, preaching, music production, singing and song writing. However, he is best known as a musician of contemporary and progressive gospel music. Deitrick is also recognized as one of the “Preachers of L.A.” (2013 – present) cast members. Deitrick Haddon has been accumulating his net worth being active in show business since 1995.

Interested in the total size of Deitrick Haddon’s net worth? It has been estimated that his current wealth (2015) is as much as $10 million, accumulated over 20 years from his various activities in the entertainment industry.

Deitrick Haddon Net Worth $10 Million

To begin with, Haddon started his career with the music band Voices of Unity, which released a number of albums, including “Come Into This House” (1995), “Live the Life” (1997), “This Is My Story” (1998), “Chain Breaker” (1999) and others. Yet, only a few of the albums managed to appear on the Billboard Gospel Top 100. Still, Deitrick decided to pursue a solo career performing Christian R&B music, releasing his “Lost & Found” (2002) album which peaked in the 5th position of the previously mentioned chart. Afterwards, one successful album followed the other. Many of his albums topped the Billboard Gospel Top 100, to give examples, “Crossroads” (2004), “Church on the Moon” (2011), “R.E.D.” (Restoring Everything Damaged) (2013) and “Deitrick Haddon’s LXW” (League of Xtraordinary Worshippers) (2014). Other studio albums also took high positions on the above mentioned chart: “7 Days” (2006) reached the 4th position whereas “Revealed” (2008) took the second. These successful studio albums and a number of singles increased the outright size of Deitrick Haddon’s net worth, plus the artist has released three live albums, three compilation albums and two soundtrack albums. All of them except two live albums appeared on the Billboard Gospel Top 100.

To add more points to this, Deitrick Haddon alongside his brother Gerald co-produced Vanessa Bell Armstrong’s studio album “Brand New Day” (2007). This has also added sums to the total size of Haddon’s net worth.

Another source of Deitrick Haddon’s popularity and net worth is television. Since 2013, he has been in the main cast of the reality television series “Preachers of L.A.” (2013–present) aired on the Oxygen network. In the series the lives of six preachers (three bishops and three pastors) who reside in Los Angeles are shown. The series focuses on their lives at home as well as their work in the church. Due to the high audience ratings engendered, several spin-offs in Dallas, Atlanta and New York have been launched.

Finally, in the personal life of the musician and preacher, Deitrick Haddon has married twice. He married his first wife Damita Haddon in 1996, however, they divorced in 2011. Now, Haddon has been married to Dominique McTyer Haddon since 2013 The family has two daughters who were born respectively in 2011 and 2014 as well as the son who was born in summer of 2015. The family resides in Los Angeles, USA.
