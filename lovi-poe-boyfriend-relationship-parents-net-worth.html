Lovi Poe Is A Filipino Actress; How Much Is Her Net Worth?

Lovi Poe is a Filipino actress with prominent roles in movies and tv series like Love to Love (2006), Bakekang (2006-07), Gumapang ka sa lusak (2010), My Valentine Girls (2011), Captain Barbell (2011), Shake Rattle & Roll XV (2014), Someone to Watch Over Me (2016-17), Tadhana (2017-19), Anak ni Waray vs. Anak ni Biday (2020), and others.

In her acting career of more than two and a half-decades, she has received the award for Breakthrough Artist of the Year at the SOP Music Awards and FAMAS award for Best Actress in 2010 for Sagrada Familia.

Despite such success, Lovi has not placed her net worth details in the face of the media till the date.

Lovi Poe's Parents & Education

Lovi Poe, who stands at the height of 5 feet and 5 inches tall, was born as Lourdes Virginia Moran-Poe on 11 February 1989 in Quezon City, Philippines.

She is the only daughter of her parents, Fernando Poe Jr. and Rowena Moran. Her father, Fernando, is an actor, director, and politician, and her mother is an actress and beauty queen.

Lovi is a graduate of Miriam College with a degree in International Academy of Management and Economics. Before that, she pursued her education at Colegio San Agustin.
