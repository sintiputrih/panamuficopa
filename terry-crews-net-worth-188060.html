Terry Crews Net Worth

Terry Crews is an actor and former NFL player born on July 30, 1968 in Flint, MI. He is best known for his role as Chris's stingy father on the TV series Everybody Hates Chris, and for his starring role on the Golden Globe-winning comedy Brooklyn Nine-Nine. He has also hosted the FOX series World's Funniest Fails and has appeared in films such as White Chicks, Norbit, Get Smart, Bridesmaids, Blended, and The Expendables.

About

Actor and former NFL player who became known for playing Chris's stingy father on the TV series Everybody Hates Chris. He also landed a starring role on the Golden Globe-winning comedy Brooklyn Nine-Nine and hosted the FOX series World's Funniest Fails. His film credits include White Chicks, Norbit, Get Smart, Bridesmaids, Blended and The Expendables. 

Before Fame

He attended Western Michigan University, where he played football. He spent a handful of seasons in the NFL, appearing in 32 games between 1991 and 1995. 

Trivia

He was on the NBC Celebrity reality competition Stars Earn Stripes and starred in a series of Old Spice commercials. He played President Camacho in Idiocracy.

Family Life

He had five children with Rebecca Crews, who he married in the 1990s.

Associated With

He starred as Hale Caesar in the testosterone-fueled Expendables films alongside Sylvester Stallone.
