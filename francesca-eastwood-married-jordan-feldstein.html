Francesca Eastwood and Jordan Feldstein annul marriage after one week

Francesca Eastwood and Jonah Hill's brother, Jordan Feldstein, are to annul their week-long marriage, according to new reports.The pair, who "hadn't been dating that long", filed a marriage license with the Clark County Recorder's Office on Sunday 17 November.Francesca and Jordan, who manages Hollywood stars including Adam Levine, Maroon 5 and Robin Thicke, exchanged vows at the Simple Wedding Chapel in Las Vegas. The idea of getting married was apparently fuelled by alcohol, sources close to the duo told TMZ. 

The 20-year-old model and daughter of Clint Eastwood appeared to have immediate regrets the following day, and was pictured not wearing a wedding ring, according to the publication.At the time of the nuptials, the news of Francesca and Jordan's marriage clearly surprised friends and family. "They haven't even been dating that long, but when in Vegas," an insider told
