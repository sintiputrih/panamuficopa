Overall Meaning

In Glenn Lewis's song Fall Again, the singer expresses his deep desire for his lover. The lyrics talk about the intense love Glenn feels for his partner, and how he cannot bear to be separated from her even for a moment. The opening lines of the song "Feels like a fire that burns in my heart, every single moment that we spend apart, I need you around for every day to start, I haven't left you alone" showcase his deep longing and love for his partner. Glenn wants to be with his lover all the time, and being away from her is like a fire burning within him.


The song also talks about how the couple has faced several ups and downs in their relationship, but they have overcome these issues together. In the lines "We fought in a battle, nobody won, and now we face a mountain to be overcome, you can't turn away, the past is said and done, I need us to carry on," the singer talks about how difficult times have brought them together and made their love stronger. They have to face the hurdles together and move on.


In conclusion, the song is all about a deep and passionate love that cannot be defined in words. The singer not only wants to spend time with his partner but fall in love with her again like they did when they first met.
