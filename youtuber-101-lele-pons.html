YouTuber 101: Lele Pons - CelebMix

This is your go-to guide for YouTuber, Lele Pons.

When is her birthday?

Lele’s birthday is June 25th, 1996.

What is Lele’s most popular video?

Her most popular video to date with 32,335,367 views is ‘Super Mario Run’.

What else do I need to know about Lele?

Lele is a Venezuelan and American internet personality and actress, most known for her YouTube videos. She began her career on video platform ‘Vine’, where she hit 8.4 billion loops, making her the most looped person on Vine. Lele has starred in the first 7 minutes of American Horror TV Show ‘Scream’, guest starred in Joey Graceffa’s YouTube Red series ‘Escape The Night’, has her own Amazon movie called ‘We Love You‘ and if you look closely enough, you will also see her in a scene of ‘The Space Between Us’. Lele released a book in 2016 called ‘Surviving High School‘, where she gives her followers an insight look on her time in High School. In February 2017, Lele was announced as Covergirl’s new ambassador and has walked the Dolce & Gabbana catwalk for their Milan Fashion Show week in February 2017.
