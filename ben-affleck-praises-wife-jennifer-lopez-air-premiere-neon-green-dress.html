Ben Affleck Praises Wife Jennifer Lopez at 'Air' Premiere

Jennifer Lopez and Ben Affleck were absolute couple goals at the premiere of Air last night.

The pair joined other stars at the Regency Village Theatre in Los Angeles to celebrate Affleck's new film, which he directed and stars in.

Mr. and Mrs. Affleck walked the red carpet arm in arm in their elegant looks. Lopezâ€™s long-sleeved turtleneck dress by Antonio Grimaldi was sheer white with shimmering crystals at the top, and neon green at the bottom. She accessorized only with a silver clutch, diamond earrings, and silver-and-diamond rings. The Marry Me star opted for bronzy eye makeup, a glossy lip, and a relaxed bun with wavy strands of hair framing her face. Her glazed donut manicure perfectly matched the top of her gown.

Axelle/Bauer-Griffin//Getty Images

Lopez smiled widely at her husband beside her. The actor and director looked dapper in a navy corduroy double-breasted suit by Thom Sweeney worn over a light blue dress shirt and a textured navy tie. He finished the look with brown dress shoes.

Axelle/Bauer-Griffin//Getty Images

At the event, Affleck praised his wife for supporting him during his film project. "She's brilliant," Affleck told Entertainment Tonight, "and [she] helps me in every conceivable way."

He previously gushed about Lopez in an interview with The Hollywood Reporter, crediting the singer with helping him and co-producer Matt Damon with the story for the film. "She is incredibly knowledgeable about the way fashion evolves through the culture as a confluence of music, sports, entertainment and dance," Affleck said.

Gilbert Flores//Getty Images

The biographical sports drama chronicles the origin of the Air Jordan shoes, and follows the journey of a Nike employee who seeks to strike a business deal with then rookie basketball player Michael Jordan. The film stars Affleck, Damon, Jason Bateman, Marlon Wayans, Chris Messina, Chris Tucker, and Viola Davis.

Air is set to hit theaters on April 5.
