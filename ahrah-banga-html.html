Ahrah Banga - Age, Family, Bio

Reality television star and TikTok star known for the reality television series Bad Boys. His ahrah66banga Instagram page features album art, updates about his music and more. In September 2023, he posted a series of videos recorded at Times Square to promote a new song.

Before Fame

He grew up in a close-knit family with the dream of becoming a content creator. He made his break into the spotlight on Bad Boys.

Trivia

He has an interest in music and released the song "Lick Back." In July 2021, he was shot five times on his way to work from his mother's home. His mother saved his life through CPR.

Family Life

He was born in Montgomery County, Maryland and raised in Northeast Washington DC. He is close with his mother Antoinette and with his aunts. He has four brothers and a sister. He is the oldest child. His birth name is Andre Brown Jr. He was raised by his mother and grandmother. His father, Mustafa Zulu was imprisoned when Ahrah was one year old. His father was 16 at the time.

Associated With

He co-starred in Bad Boys with Jonathan Wright, Orlando Brown, and Milan Christopher.
