Get To Know Billy Kametz Wife Or Girlfriend, Family, Cause Of Death

Billy Kametz, the voice actor, died on 12 June 2022. He is known for voicing many widely known characters. The news came as a shock to many anime fans.

A GoFundMe page was set up shortly after Billy broke the news that he suffered from a chronic illness. The page’s organizer, his event agent A. Jinnie McManus, shared the awful news, along with the actor’s obituary, in an update on the page, and was kind to his admirers.

“Anyone who has met Billy Kametz will tell you he is one of the warmest, kindest, most-sincere human beings on this earth,” the page said. “Quick to laugh with his infectious smile, he brings joy to everyone around him — whether that is a room full of friends and family, gracious fans at conventions, or, most recently, the nurses and doctors who attended to him in the hospital.”

“Most importantly, I hope you know how appreciated you all were and how much strength you provided. Billy and I would regularly share thoughts of disbelief and joy at the outpouring of generosity you all gave him,” the GoFundMe update read. “Know that he left this world peacefully, knowing how loved he is and always will be. I miss my friend terribly. But it comforts me that his legacy will continue with his work and all of the fans who are even now discovering what an extraordinary man he was”.

Fans wondered about his grieving family and if he leave behind a wife or a girlfriend. We attempt to cover his love life along with other details below in this article.

So, keep reading to learn more about him.

Billy Kametz Cause Of Death

Billy Kametz fought stage four colon cancer, which became the cause of his death. On 24 April 2022, he shared his diagnosis.

He indicated at the time that he was “doing OK” and that he was surrounded by a supportive team of doctors and specialists. Billy said he had already begun chemotherapy and was having radiation treatments on various parts of his spine.

“It sucks, it’s just a freak of nature thing that happened that I just gotta deal with now and we’re gonna do it,” Billy said, later adding, “I’m staying really positive, and that’s the biggest reason that I wanted to make this video. It’s just to let you know that I have so much support, so much positivity surrounding me… I just want to let you guys know that I’m not going to quit, I’m not going to stop fighting, and I’m gonna get through this”.

Did Billy Kametz Leave Behind A Wife Or A Girlfriend?

To cut to the chase, no, Billy Kametz didn’t have a wife. He wasn’t married which is confirmed by his obituary, as it doesn’t mention him having a wife. However, there were rumors of Billy once dating another voice actor Erica Lindbeck. One follower tweeted while tagging both, “Holy shit, they’re dating”.

On 12 June 2022, Erica posted, “I will be in love with you for the rest of my life”.

Previously, a fan asked in February 2021, “I’ve been wondering this since I saw you on one of his streams but are you dating each other?” In December 2021, a nosy fan asked, “Erica and Billy sitting on a bench… Are you guys close, like really close, like dating or even further?”

Another grieving fan posted after his death, “Love and prayers to your family Billy and to Erica”. Fans saw them together for the last time when they were streaming together and Bill appeared happy and smiled during the whole thing.

Erica is a voice actress born on 29 May 1992. She hailed from Boston, Massachusetts.

Erica is known for voicing Black Cat from the 2018 Spider-Man video game, Loona from Helluva Boss, Cassie Cage from Mortal Kombat, the Barbie doll, and many others. She and Mela Lee hosted the show Lindbeck and Lee with other local voice actors as guests.
