Who Is Logan Thomas Wife Brandie Thomas?

Logan Thomas is an American football tight end who plies his trade in the National Football League for the Washington Football Team. Prior to joining the Washington Football Team, Logan Thomas played for the Arizona Cardinals, Miami Dolphins, Detroit Lions, New York Giants, and the Buffalo Bills.

Logan Thomas played college football for the Virginia Tech Hokies and was selected by the Arizona Cardinals in the fourth round of the 2014 NFL Draft.

Who Is Logan Thomas’ Wife Brandie Thomas?

Logan Thomas is married to a beautiful American called Brandie Thomas and they are blessed with five sons.

Brandie Thomas was born and grew up in Cincinnati and she is known to many just as the wife of the American football tight end, Logan Thomas. Brandie Thomas is living a private life and as such, there is little information about her.

Brandie Thomas works at “The Thomas Estate”, which is a wedding venue developed by Brandie and Logan Thomas.
