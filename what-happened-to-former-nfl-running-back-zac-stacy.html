The Rise and Fall of NFL Running Back Zac Stacy

Former NFL running back Zac Stacy was sentenced to six months in jail in Florida after pleading guilty to battery in relation to an assault on his ex-girlfriend, Kristin Evans. The incident took place in 2019 and was captured on home security cameras. Stacy, who previously played for the St. Louis Rams and the New York Jets, was remanded into custody and later sentenced, marking a significant and distressing turn in his post-NFL life.

Assault on Ex-Girlfriend

The incident, which occurred in 2019, involved Stacy allegedly punching his ex-girlfriend and throwing her into a TV while their infant son was nearby. The video evidence was entered in court, and Stacy eventually pleaded guilty to the battery charges. The assault, captured on security cameras, triggered a wave of attention and legal proceedings that culminated in Stacy's sentencing to jail time.

Legal Proceedings

Following the release of the video, Stacy was the subject of a manhunt, with law enforcement urging him to turn himself in. Eventually, he was arrested, and the case progressed through the legal system, ultimately resulting in Stacy's guilty plea and subsequent sentencing. The legal proceedings and the sentencing marked a significant downfall for Stacy, who had previously been known for his career as an NFL running back.

NFL Career and Retirement

Zac Stacy's NFL career began when he was drafted by the St. Louis Rams in 2013 as a 5th round pick from Vanderbilt University. He played two seasons with the Rams and then joined the New York Jets for one season in 2015. Unfortunately, due to injuries, Stacy retired from the NFL in 2017. After his retirement, he attempted to continue his football career in other leagues, trying out for the Saskatchewan Roughriders in 2018 before joining the Memphis Express of the Alliance of American Football in 2019, which folded before completing its first season.

Impact of the Incident

The assault on his ex-girlfriend and subsequent legal proceedings have undoubtedly had a profound impact on Stacy's personal and professional life. Once known for his achievements in professional football, he is now facing the consequences of his actions, with a jail sentence and the public scrutiny that accompanies such a high-profile case. The incident has not only affected Stacy personally but has also overshadowed his sporting career and has likely had broader implications for his public image and reputation.
