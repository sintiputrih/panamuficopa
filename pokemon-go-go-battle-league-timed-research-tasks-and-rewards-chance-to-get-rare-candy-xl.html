GO Battle League Timed Research tasks and rewards, Chance to Get Rare Candy XL

Trainers, if you’re a fan of Pokémon GO’s GO Battle League, we’ve got some exciting news for you. The Adventures Abound event has kicked off, and it’s offering you an unbeatable deal—a free pass to an exclusive, battle-themed Timed Research in Pokémon GO. Yes, you heard it right. Absolutely free.

This special Pokémon GO Timed Research aims to document your hard-fought victories throughout the season. Each page of the research requires a whopping 100 wins to complete. And the loot? Stellar rewards like Stardust, Elite Fast TMs, Elite Charged TMs, and Rare Candy XL. This Timed Research in Pokémon GO is available from Friday, September 1, 2023, at 1:00 p.m. to Friday, September 15, 2023, at 1:00 p.m. PDT.

Before diving into the nitty-gritty of tasks and rewards, let’s chat about why you should even bother with this Pokémon GO Timed Research. Those 100 wins per research page aren’t going to be easy to achieve. But the end-game rewards like Elite Fast TM after 400 wins and Elite Charged TM after 500 wins, plus Rare Candy XL for every 100 wins, make it a challenging yet rewarding endeavor.
