Cedrick Hardman Net Worth, Income, Salary, Earnings, Biography

He played defensive end for the San Francisco 49ers and Oakland Raiders of the National Football League, as well as the Oakland Invaders of the United States Football League. Cedrick Ward Hardman was born on October 4, 1948, in Houston, Texas, and is a retired American football defensive end. Hardman’s professional football career spanned thirteen years, beginning in 1970 with the National Football League and concluding in 1983 as a player/coach with the United States Football League’s Oakland Invaders. Hardman is the 49ers’ all-time leader in sacks in a season with a total of 22.
