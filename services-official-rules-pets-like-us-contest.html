Official Rules for the “Pets Like Us” Contest

NO PURCHASE REQUIRED TO ENTER OR WIN.

1. Eligibility: Open to legal residents of the 50 contiguous United States and Washington DC who as of the time of entry are 18 years of age or older (and at least age of majority in state). Contest is subject to all applicable federal, state and local laws and regulations and is void where prohibited by law.

4. How to Enter:

a. To enter the “Pets Like Us” Contest (the “Contest”), read the Contest rules and follow the instructions, including, without limitation:

· Take a photo of your celebrity-resembling pet;

· On your valid, public (i.e. not “private”) Instagram account, post the photograph on Instagram with the @UsWeekly handle, the hastag “#PetsLikeUsContest” and add the name of the celebrity your pet looks like in the caption;

· The photo you share should only consist of your pet photo (no celebrity photos as part of your entry please).

(each such photograph and entry, a “Submission”).

Limit one entry per person, regardless of the method used to enter.

If you do not have an Instagram account, visit www.instagram.com and register in accordance with the enrollment instructions for a free Instagram account. By submitting an entry to the aforementioned handle and hash tag, the entrant is confirming that they have read and agree to comply with these official rules and all other contest requirements.

The entry period is 9:00 AM Eastern Standard Time (“EST”) on 4/5/2016 and ends at 11:59:59 PM EST on 4/7/2016.

b. Limit one entry per person. All entries must be received by the close of entry period. Participants may not enter with multiple Instagram accounts nor may entrants use any other device or artifice to enter multiple times during entry period or under multiple identities. Any participant who attempts to enter during entry period under multiple identities or uses any device or artifice to enter multiple times will be disqualified and forfeits any and all prizes won, in Sponsor’s discretion. Sponsor reserves the right, in its sole discretion, to cancel the Contest if it does not receive a sufficient number of eligible and qualified Submissions.

In the event of a tie, the entry with the higher score first in the similarity to celebrity category, then in the creativity category, then in the quality of photo category will be declared a winner.

b. The winners will be notified by adding a comment from @UsWeekly to the winner’s photo submission on the winner’s Instagram account on or around 4/8/2016.
