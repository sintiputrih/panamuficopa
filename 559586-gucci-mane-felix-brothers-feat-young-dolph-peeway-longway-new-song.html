Felix Brothers

Gucci Mane maybe incarcerated, but that doesn't stop him from pushing out new content on the reg. After releasing Trap House 4 a couple weeks ago, Gucci Mane and his 1017 Records have another joint project in the works for us titled Felix Brothers, with Young Dolph & Peeway Longway, and today they decided to share with us the title track for it.

While this joint project is set for a June 18th release date, Guwop will follow that up with his 4th & final project a week later on July 28th titled Mr Clean & The Middle Man. If keeping track, that's 4 projects in the month of July alone.

Stream this hard street cut below, and look for more Gucci leaks later this month.
