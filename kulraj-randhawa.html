Biography

Kulraj Randhawa was born in India on May 16, 1980. Best known for her title role in the 2004-2005 Indian Zee TV series Kareena Kareena, she also appeared in such Punjabi and Hindi films as Mannat and Chintuji. She co-starred with Bollywood actor Rishi Kapoor in a 2009 Hindi film titled Chintu Ji. She began appearing in Kareena Kareena when she was twenty-four years old; two years later, she starred in the film Mannat.
