Freddie Mercury Wife: Who is Mary Austin?

Mary Austin and Freddie Mercury first met in the early 1970s, and their friendship endured long after their romance was over.

Many people want to know how and what happened to Freddie Mercury’s children if he had any. Find out here what caused Freddie’s passing.

Freddie Mercury Age

 He passed away in 1991 at the age of 45.

Freddie Mercury Biography

Freddie Mercury, who was born on September 5th, 1946,

He was raised in a middle-class Parsi family from Stone Town, Tanzania, where he was born. He is a citizen of the United Kingdom and practices Zoroastrianism. His heritage is Indian Parsi, and Virgo is his sun sign.

In Panchgani, Maharashtra, India, at the Boarding School, he completed his early schooling. Following that, he enrolled at the Isleworth Polytechnic College in Isleworth, England, and later at the Ealing Art College, where he eventually earned his diploma.

He was always considerably more interested in extracurricular activities than in academics, even as a young child.

Freddie Mercury Wife

Freddie never had a valid marriage. The singer was engaged to Mary Austin for a protracted period of time. When she was 19 and Freddie was 24, they first connected through their bandmate Brian May in 1969.

Mercury and Austin’s relationship developed, and a short while later, they reportedly moved in together. In the end, the couple decided against getting married.

Freddie Mercury Wife: Who is Mary Austin?

Mary Austin was one of the most significant figures in his life, despite the fact that she was very different from the flashy frontman of Queen. The deaf parents of the soft-spoken working-class girl raised her, and she stood at Freddie Mercury’s side until his passing in 1991.

How did Freddie Mercury Meet Mary Austin?

They started dating, and Mary moved in with Freddie in West Kensington during their relationship.

Freddie and Austin had fallen in love and started living together in the early days of Queen. When Mary was 23, Freddie had asked her to marry him but, in 1976, he had to come clean to her about questioning his sexuality and having been unfaithful to her. Mary had been supportive of Freddie throughout his career and stayed around until the end of his life.

When Did Freddie Mercury And Mary Broke up?

While there had been rumors about Freddie’s sexuality, the constant presence of Mary meant his sexuality was not seriously questioned for some time.

Did Freddie Mercury Leave Money For Mary Austin?

When Freddie Mercury died in 1991, he left behind a remarkable legacy of rock and roll, thousands of broken hearts and almost half of his fortune to his ex-fiancé Mary Austin.

Freddie Mercury had once said, “All my lovers asked me why they couldn’t replace Mary, but it’s simply impossible. The only friend I’ve got is Mary, and I don’t want anybody else. To me, she was my common-law wife. To me, it was a marriage…We believe in each other. That’s enough for me. I couldn’t fall in love with a man the same way as I have with Mary.”

Where is Mary Austin currently?

Mary had to combat jealous rivals and was even abandoned by Freddie’s bandmates after she inherited his fortune and was given the responsibility to bury his ashes in a clandestine location.

Today, Austin lives a quiet life in the London mansion she decorated and inherited from Freddie even as fans make a pilgrimage to Freddie’s London home.

Does Freddie Mercury have a child?

Freddie Mercury never married, and he didn’t have any children. He had a long-term relationship with a woman, Mary Austin, and they lived together for several years until he came out as gay to her in 1976, having started a romantic affair with US record exec David Minns.
