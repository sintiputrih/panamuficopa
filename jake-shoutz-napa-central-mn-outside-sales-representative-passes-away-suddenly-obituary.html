Jake Shoutz, NAPA Central MN outside sales representative, passes away suddenly: obituary

Grove City MN – The news of the passing of Jake Shoutz, an esteemed NAPA Central MN Outside Sales Representative from Grove City, Minnesota, has left his family and friends in deep mourning. According to a heartfelt Facebook post, Jake Shoutz was honored as he peacefully departed on Thursday, November 2nd, 2023 at 11:16 AM, leaving behind a multitude of individuals who cherished his vibrant spirit and uplifting presence.

Details regarding the circumstances of Jake Shoutz’s passing have not been officially disclosed at the time of this report. Graduating from Litchfield Sr. High and holding the position of Outside Sales Representative at NAPA Central MN, Jake’s commitment extended beyond his professional realm. His involvement in various humanitarian initiatives underscored his deep-seated dedication to creating a positive impact in the world, demonstrating resilience in the face of challenges and inspiring others to persevere in the pursuit of their dreams.

Chantelle Strom posted on Facebook,

“Heaven has gained another angel today November 2nd at 11:16 AM. Jake Shoutz you will be missed by so many!! There was never a dull moment with you around, you always knew how to cheer someone up when they were down. You were one of many uncles of Graysons he loved you so much!! Now you are with all the loved ones that you have missed for so long! You are free from struggling and pain, Jake we love you so much! Already miss you like crazy. Till we see you again! Rest easy bud! You take care of things from up there and the rest of us will take care of things from here. Make sure to give Steven some love from us too, I know you guys are gonna cause some trouble up there together! I can already see it!! Love you oodles!!”

Through his active participation in community development projects and charitable organizations, Jake Shoutz exemplified a genuine concern for the welfare of others, radiating a beacon of hope for those in need and embodying the spirit of selfless service. His unwavering determination during difficult times served as a testament to his unwavering character and left an indelible mark on the lives of all those he touched.

In his unwavering commitment and profound sense of purpose, Jake Shoutz not only inspired those around him but also instilled in generations to come the importance of steadfast dedication and the pursuit of excellence in every endeavor, even amidst a rapidly changing world. He will be remembered as a dedicated and cherished individual whose legacy of resilience and compassion will continue to inspire all who had the privilege of knowing him.
