Neil Patrick Harris | Net Worth & Income

Net Worth

He has excelled on both the small and silver screen, earning both fame and fortune to live a standard lifestyle.

Though most of the dollars in his fortune can be credited to his How I met Your Mother salary, his film roles also helped him garner sizable and consistent income.

As mentioned before, he has appeared in more than 38 films to date, and the number speaks for itself.

The box office collection of the films in which Harris has starred in a lead and supporting role are over $361,171,206 and $2,315,871,368 globally, respectively.

Income

How I Met Your Mother Earning

Neil Patrick Harris was the highest-earning cast member in the show pulling close to half a million dollars per episode during the final seasons.

While his initial season salary from the show is still a mystery, in the fifth season, his per episode salary was $150,000.

Considering 24 episodes a season on average, that’s like $3.6 million for the season five.

In 2012, after a salary negotiation with the producers, Harris’s salary was bumped by $50,000 per episode, making it a total of $200,000.

Again considering 24 episodes, Harris made $4.8 million in the seventh season.

For the eighth season, Harris’s salary was $225,000 and $5.4 million in total.

And for the ninth and the final season of the show, Harris’s salary was a staggering $400,000.

That’s more than double of what he was making initially.

In total, his salary from the final season of the show was a whopping $9.6 million.

Neil Patrick Harris | Lifestyle & Vacations

Lifestyle

With a massive net worth of $50 million and a continuously flowing stream of money, Neil Patrick Harris leads a comfortable lifestyle.

Harris is one of the most successful gay men out in the American entertainment industry.

He publicly came out as gay in November 2006.

However, from 1997 to 1998, he had dated American actress Christine Taylor.

In an interview with Howard Stern in 2008, he revealed how his relationship with Christine helped him realize his true sexuality.

Similarly, in September 2007, he attended Emmy Awards with his fiancé David Burtka. The couple later confirmed their relationship.

In an interview on The Ellen, Patrick revealed that his relationship with David began in 2004.

Harris and David announced that they were expecting a twin via a surrogate mother on August 12, 2010.

In October 2010, their daughter Harper Grace and son Gideon Scott were born.

Harris and David announced their engagement via Twitter after the Marriage Equality Act in New York was passed on June 24, 2011.

They also revealed that they had proposed to each other five years earlier but kept it to themselves until same-sex marriage became legal.

The couple married on September 8, 2014, in Italy.

Sir Elton John performed at their reception.

Neil Patrick Harris | Career

Born in Albuquerque, New Mexico, Neil was raised alongside his elder brother by lawyer parents.

He began acting as a child but also managed to attend high school.

He graduated with high grades in 1991.

In 1988, Neil appeared in the comedy/sci-fi film Purple People Eater.

Even though he had made a good name in the film industry, his real breakthrough came with Doogie Houses, M.D. The show was extremely popular among fans and ran for four-season.

The show also garnered him a Golden Globe nomination.

In 2005, he landed on How I Met Your Mother as a lead which ran for nine seasons, further inflating his fame and fortune.
