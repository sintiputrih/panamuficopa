Frank Morrison Spillane (March 9, 1918 – July 17, 2006), best known as Mickey Spillane, was an American mystery novelist who created Mike Hammer, his trademark detective character. His novels have sold over 225 million copies across the world. Spillane was responsible for seven of the top fifteen best-selling fiction titles in the United States in 1980.
