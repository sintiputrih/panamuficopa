David Heckard Biography

David Heckard is an American journalist working for News 13 Orlando as a correspondent meteorologist. Additionally, he hosts the program, “Weather on the Ones” forecast on weekends and middays. Before joining Spectrum News, he was working at WZVN-TV as a weekend evening meteorologist.

David Heckard Education

Heckard attended Purdue University and earned a bachelor of science degree in atmospheric science. While at Purdue, he interned at WEHT and at WLS-TV in Chicago. In addition, he was a staff writer for the university student newspaper, The Purdue Exponent.

He has been designated a Certified Broadcast Meteorologist of the American Meteorological Society.

David Heckard News13

He began his television career as the weekend meteorologist at WXVT in Greenville, Mississippi. Prior to Florida, Heckard was the weekend and 4 p.m. meteorologist at WEHT in Evansville, Indiana from 2006-2013.

In Evansville, he covered all kinds of extreme weather, from blizzards and ice storms to major tornado outbreaks. He also did field coverage for the landfall of Hurricane Isaac in 2012.

Heckard had experiences of the hurricanes in Florida. He learned about the different seasons during hurricanes and this developed his knowledge in hurricane dealings. He mainly focused on forecasting the  Hermine and Matthew. In addition to that, Heckard had also worked in the Fort Myers area covering two tornados in the area.
