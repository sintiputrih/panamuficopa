PSG Star, Kylian Mbappe Dating Ines Rau, The 1st Transgender Model To Have Been Featured On Playboy

After splitting from Emma Smet, PSG and France striker Kylian Mbappe has reportedly been dating famous model Ines Rau for quite some time.

According to Corriere Dello Sports, Mbappe was spotted with Rau at the Cannes Film Festival, and then on a yacht where the footballer was seen carrying his girlfriend.

Mbappe’s personal life is largely unknown. However, rumors about his new girlfriend gained traction after an image of him lifting Rau was published in the Italian press.

About Ines Rau

Rau is a French model of Algerian descent who became the first transgender model to appear on the cover of Playboy magazine in 2017. She debuted in Playboy in 2014, where she posed naked for a special section titled Evolution.

Rau, who aspires to work in Hollywood, began transitioning at the age of 17, but did not come out to the public until she was 24.

“I went a long time without saying anything about being transgender,” Rau says in the accompanying interview. “I dated a lot and almost forgot about it.” I was afraid of never finding a boyfriend and being perceived as odd. Then I thought, “You know, you should just be yourself.”
