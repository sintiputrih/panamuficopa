Marc Guiu Height: How Tall Is Marc Guiu?

Marc Guiu height-Spanish professional footballer, Marc Guiu Paz was born on January 4, 2006, in Granollers, Barcelona, Spain.

How tall is Marc Guiu?

Marc Guiu stands at a height of 6ft 2 inches or 187 centimeters/1.87 meters and weighs 75kg.

Marc Guiu career

Guiu’s career began with the Penya Barcelonista Sant Celoni, a supporter group for La Liga team Barcelona.

He later joined Barcelona themselves for the 2013–14 season, advanced through the La Masia program, and received his first call-up to the senior team in June 2023, ahead of exhibition games against Vissel Kobe of Japan and Celta de Vigo.

In the later game, Guiu made his unofficial debut for Barcelona by replacing Robert Lewandowski at halftime.

He was once again called up to the senior team on October 11, 2023, for a La Liga match against Granada, although he ultimately did not play.

He was rated one of the top players born in 2006 internationally by the English daily The Guardian in the same month.

As a 79th-minute substitute, Guiu made his senior competitive debut on October 22 against Athletic Bilbao, scoring the game-winning goal in just 23 seconds with his second touch.

He made his La Liga debut for Barcelona at the age of 17 years and 291 days, making him the league’s youngest and quickest scorer.

Guiu is a “classic ‘9’, a specialist in the area who exploits his physical power,” according to Barcelona youth coach Iván Carrasco.

His high ball game, pace, strength, and work rate are all good. Guiu was referred to be a striker who is “more efficient in the box” by Spanish football expert Guillem Balague.
