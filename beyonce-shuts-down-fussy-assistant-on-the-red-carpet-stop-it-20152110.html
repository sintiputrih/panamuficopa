Beyonce Shuts Down Fussy Assistant on the Red Carpet: &quot;Stop It&quot;

A different form of shutting it down! Beyonce nonchalantly scolded an assistant who was fussing over her dress on the red carpet of the TIDAL X: 1020 event on Tuesday, Oct. 20.

A video shows the global singing sensation, 34, beaming before photographers at Barclays Center in Brooklyn, NYC, where she looks gorgeous in an oxblood gown with a plunging neckline accompanied by a matching fur stole. The songstress topped her look with a high genie-style ponytail that cascaded to one side.

In the clip, photographers snap away at the beauty, as Beyonce’s stylist tweaks her outfit. The assistant appears concerned about how Beyonce’s dress drapes down her body, especially since her bodice daringly exposes part of her cleavage. As the employee fusses over Bey, one shutterbug is heard saying, “Right here, queen,” to grab Beyonce’s attention.

The superstar looks over, grins, and casually tells her assistant to “stop it” as she resumes posing on the red carpet. The assistant immediately stops, scurries over to the side, and allows Beyonce to continue reveling in her spotlight.

Beyonce was at the event on Tuesday to support Jay Z‘s TIDAL, and she even gave a raunchy performance of “Feeling Myself” alongside collaborator Nicki Minaj. She also performed “Holy Grail” alongside her husband.
