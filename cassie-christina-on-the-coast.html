HGTV Star Dishes on BFF (EXCLUSIVE)

Christina Hall from 'Christina on the Coast' spoke with 'Distractify' about her friendship with Cassie, the founder of the PR company CMZPR.

If you’re a fan of Christina Hall and her HGTV show Christina on the Coast, then you’ve likely seen her publicist, Cassie (Zebisch) Schienle, pop up on a few episodes.

The duo has actually known each other for more than two decades, and Cassie is even the godmother of Christina’s eldest child, Taylor El Moussa. Needless to say, these badass businesswomen are friendship goals.

"We've literary done everything in life together," Christina shared in an exclusive interview with Distractify ahead of the mid-Season 4 premiere of Christina on the Coast. "We have this saying, C and C, [because] we always knew we would work together when we were older." Clearly, it was friendship at first sight.

Given their history, we weren't surprised when Cassie tasked Christina with helping her to reveal the gender of her second baby (yes, she's pregnant). Keep reading to find out more about the dynamic duo.

Christina Hall and Cassie were once chased by a swarm of bees while filming 'Christina on the Coast'.

As two friends who have basically spent their entire lives together, we imagine Christina and Cassie have some pretty exciting stories to tell. And we assumed right. Christina revealed during an exclusive interview with Distractify that while filming an episode of Christina on the Coast, the besties encountered something they never thought they’d run into – a swarm of bees!

“We were filming this scene, and as we’re filming, all of a sudden, we hear this buzzing, and literally like 1,000 bees came out,” Christina lightheartedly shared. The TV personality says they both immediately ran in different directions, “like a scene out of I Love Lucy, back and forth zig zagging.” I guess faulty wiring isn’t the only thing Christina comes across when renovating homes!

Article continues below advertisement

Aside from having to confront some unfriendly creatures on set, Christina says she and Cassie will often try to surprise each other while the cameras are rolling.

During Season 4 of Christina on the Coast, Cassie surprises Christina by telling her she's pregnant with her second child, and wants her to assist with the gender reveal. The news left Christina speechless, and of course, overjoyed. But we imagine it wasn't easy being the only one, aside from her husband Josh Hall, knowing whether Cassie would be having a boy or girl.

Spoiler alert: Cassie is having a boy!

Christina previously renovated Cassie's childhood home.

The very first episode of Christina on the Coast featured Cassie’s residence in Anaheim Hills, Calif. "I saw a meme today that said, 'a friend knocks on your door, but a best friend walks in and eats your food,' and that’s us," Cassie previouly told House Beautiful of her relationship with Christina.

Article continues below advertisement

"We’ve gone to junior high, high school, and college together — we even lived together in college — and we’ve been through everything together," she continued. "I consider her family."

Cassie said it felt natural to film alongside her BFF. "Christina and I have such good chemistry in real life, and we just did our thing, like we do every day together," she explained. "At this point, [shooting with HGTV] is just part of my life, so the cameras are normal."

In fact, the most difficult thing for Cassie turned out to be "trusting the process" as her longtime pal gutted the kitchen and dining room.

"Christina and I have very similar tastes, and I knew she wouldn’t do something she wouldn’t want in her own home in mine," the L.A. native acknowledged. "I’ve always been a huge champion of Christina."

Cassie from 'Christina on the Coast' owns a PR company.

You can hire the brunette beauty’s firm, CMZPR, to help with media relations, crisis management, and brand partnerships. Cassie's newest client is the XFL, a company co-owned by Dwayne "The Rock" Johnson. She previously worked as the senior communications director for AEG, a leading sports and entertainment conglomerate.

In July 2020, Cassie and a few of her colleagues launched a new venture called reBUILD20 in response to the COVID-19 pandemic. The enterprise, which catered to clients who relied on in-person consumer interaction, developed "creative and actionable operations plans" that factor in the ever-changing protocols related to the health crisis.

On top of running a business, Cassie is also expecting her second child with husband James Schienle. The professional chef proposed in March 2020, shortly before coronavirus lockdowns went into effect, and the two tied the knot on March 6, 2021. Shortly after wedding, the couple welcomed their first child.

"When you realize you want to spend the rest of your life with someone, you want the rest of your life to start as soon as possible," Cassie wrote when announcing the engagement on Instagram. "I love you so much and can’t wait to spend forever with you."

In an interview with HGTV, Cassie added, "James is the person I’ve waited my whole life for, he is kind, smart, cares so much about everyone around him, and I can’t wait for what is to come! We are beyond excited and grateful for the next chapter in our lives together."

To watch Christina's reaction to the big news and see major home transformations, tune in to Christina on the Coast Season 4, Part 2 on June 29, 2023, with new episodes dropping Thursdays at 9 p.m. ET on HGTV.
