When and Where is the 2024 PGA Championship?

The 2024 PGA Championship is set to take place at the Valhalla Golf Club in Louisville, Kentucky from May 13 to May 19.

This prestigious event is eagerly anticipated by golf enthusiasts and will mark the fourth time that Valhalla Golf Club has hosted the PGA Championship.

The championship promises to be a thrilling spectacle, bringing together top golfers from around the world to compete on this renowned course designed by Jack Nicklaus.

Valhalla Golf Club: A Historic Venue

Valhalla Golf Club, designed by the legendary Jack Nicklaus, opened its doors in 1986 and quickly established itself as one of the most esteemed golf courses globally.

The club has a rich history of hosting major golf events, including the PGA Championship in 1996, 2000, and 2014, as well as the Ryder Cup in 2008.

The championship's return to this iconic venue is a testament to its exceptional quality and the thrilling competition it has consistently facilitated.

Past Championships at Valhalla Golf Club

The history of the PGA Championship at Valhalla Golf Club is illustrious.

In 1996, the club hosted its first major championship, with Mark Brooks emerging as the victor in a gripping playoff against Kentuckian Kenny Perry.

In 2000, golf legend Tiger Woods clinched the championship title after a remarkable three-hole cumulative score playoff against Bob May.

Most recently, in 2014, Rory McIlroy triumphed in challenging conditions, securing his second Wanamaker Trophy by a narrow margin over Phil Mickelson.

Future Venues

Looking ahead, the PGA Championship is scheduled to be held at the Quail Hollow Club in Charlotte, North Carolina in 2025, followed by the Aronimink Golf Club in Newtown Square, Pennsylvania in 2026.

These esteemed venues continue the tradition of hosting this prestigious tournament, showcasing the PGA Championship's commitment to presenting top-tier golf experiences across the United States.

Ticket Information and Spectator Guide

For golf enthusiasts eager to witness the 2024 PGA Championship in person, tickets and hospitality packages are available.

The official ticket exchange provides a secure platform for purchasing tickets, while the spectator guide offers valuable insights into planning the perfect visit to the championship.

Also, volunteering opportunities are available for those who wish to contribute to the success of this renowned event.

The 2024 PGA Championship and Beyond

The 2024 PGA Championship at Valhalla Golf Club represents a convergence of history, skill, and sportsmanship. As one of golf's premier events, it promises an unparalleled display of talent and determination amidst the backdrop of a storied venue.

Looking ahead, the PGA Championship's future venues, including the Baltusrol Golf Club in 2029, Congressional Country Club in 2030, and The Ocean Course in 2031, underscore the enduring legacy and excitement that this championship fosters in the world of golf.

The 2024 PGA Championship is a testament to the enduring allure of golf and the exceptional venues that host this prestigious event.

As golf enthusiasts eagerly await the championship, the Valhalla Golf Club stands ready to welcome the world's finest golfers for an unforgettable display of skill and sportsmanship.
