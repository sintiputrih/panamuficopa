    Christopher Jacot Slasher

    Christopher has made a huge fortune from his career as an actor. He is famously known for Slasher (2016), CSI: NY (2004) and Eureka (2006). Jacot has performed in a number of recurring and guest roles on various television series. Among them include Degrassi: The Next Generation, Eureka, Slasher and The Strain.

    Apart from his screen appearances, Jacot has also done a great deal of voice work. Two of his most notable roles being Johnny Storm, the Human Torch, in the animated series Fantastic Four: World’s Greatest Heroes and Maurice Vega in the video game Watch Dogs.

    Later on, he worked with Ubisoft again in Watch Dogs 2 where he voiced Dus̆an Nemec. Nemec was the main antagonist. He also provided the voice of the character Topher in Total Drama All-Stars and Pahkitew Island.
