Besides, he has worked with celebrities like Elon Musk, Jay Z, 21 Savage, Mick Jagger, Meek Mill, Bobby Shmurda, and so on.

Alex Spiri Net Worth And Salary: He Makes Six Figures Every Year

According to Profiles Info, Alex Spiro has a total net worth of $5 million. His annual salary is said to be $400,000. 

Most of his earnings come from his occupation as a lawyer and attorney. We do not know his exact legal fees. However, it likely depends on the nature and severity of the cases. 

2018 saw Alex graduate from Harvard Law School with a JD. Up until 2013, he was employed by the Manhattan District Attorney’s Office as an assistant district attorney.

His practice area covers almost every type of dispute or litigation across the globe – mainly in the US and Europe. He has overseen various complex investigations, including companies in multiple industries and issues. 

Alex has litigated cases involving white collar and regulatory issues, defamation, First Amendment law, complicated financial matters, intellectual property, bankruptcy, etc.

Alex Spiro Is More Than A Lawyer

Before entering the area of Law, Alex had studied Biopsychology and worked in an adolescent psychiatry unit at McLean Hospital. In his five years at McLean, he ran programs for children with Autism and Asperger’s syndrome. 

Alex has served on the faculty of Harvard’s Trial Advocacy Program. He is a lecturer at Harvard Law School and teaches various subjects, including trial practice, corporate privilege, evidence, and criminal law. 

Besides, he is Chairman of the Board of Harvard’s criminal justice initiative – The Fair Punishment Project. He also serves as Counsel to the Reform Alliance. 

Mr. Spiro has received several academic and professional citations, including the Albert Imlah writing award and the ACC Northeast Ethics Lawyering award. 

Alex also sits on several company boards – both private and public, like Damon Motors Inc, Cansortium Inc, and Bragg Gaming Group Inc.
