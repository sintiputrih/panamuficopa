Angela Ponce Height, Age, Boyfriend, Biography, Net worth, Family Facts

Angela Ponce (born January 18, 1991) is a Spanish Professional Model and Beauty Pageant Titleholder from Pilas, Seville. She is famous for being the first transgender woman who won “Miss Spain 2018” title.

Furthermore, the 27-year-old beauty queen earlier came out as the winner of Miss World Cadiz 2015 as well. Furthermore, her real full birth name is Ángela Ponce Camacho.

She also competed in the 67th Miss Universe pageant. Moreover, the event held on December 17, 2018, at Impact, Muang Thong Thani in Bangkok, Thailand. Ponce was not able to make it to the final round of Top 20 in the competition.

But Angela already took the heart of many people by just participating in the event. She also inspires many other women and transgender women to follow their heart.

Francesca Hung, Andrea Toscano, Nehal Chudasama, Helena Heuser, Mayra Dias etc are some of her competitors. However, Catriona Gray came out as the winner of the Miss Universe 2018.
