The Stick Sports Lounge to Close After 8 Years in Business | News

After eight years in business, The Stick Sports Lounge has announced it's closing this weekend. 

In a post on Instagram, the sports lounge's management writes: 

"Over the years, The Stick has been a vibrant and cherished part of the Reno community, providing fans with a welcoming space to come together and enjoy the excitement of various events. Unfortunately, the dynamics of Downtown Reno have shifted, and we will be closing the business after 8 fun years. We want to express our gratitude to every one of the employees for the dedication and passion they have poured into making The Stick the special place it has been. Our incredible team’s collective effort has defined the spirit of The Stick, and we are truly grateful for the memories we have created together. We also want to thank all the loyal customers who have supported the business and made it their home for so many incredible sporting events we witnessed together. These memories will last a lifetime.

This weekend is it, come and cheers with us one last time.

- Steve, Ross & The Stick Team❤️"
