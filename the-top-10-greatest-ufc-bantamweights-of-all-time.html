The Top 10 Greatest UFC Bantamweights of All-Time

When it comes to the world of mixed martial arts (MMA), the UFC (Ultimate Fighting Championship) stands as the premier organization. Within the UFC, the bantamweight division has seen some of the most talented fighters in the history of the sport. From explosive strikers to grappling wizards, the bantamweight division has always been a hotbed for thrilling and technical fights. In this article, we will delve into the top 10 greatest UFC bantamweights of all time, taking into account their skills, achievements, and impact on the sport.

1. Dominick Cruz

Dominick Cruz is widely regarded as one of the most dominant bantamweight champions in UFC history. Known for his unorthodox footwork and elusive style, Cruz has successfully defended his title on multiple occasions. His ability to dictate the pace of the fight and neutralize his opponents with his movement and accurate striking has set him apart from the rest. Despite battling injuries throughout his career, Cruz remains one of the most respected and admired fighters in the division.

2. TJ Dillashaw

TJ Dillashaw burst onto the bantamweight scene with his impressive performance on The Ultimate Fighter reality show. He quickly climbed the ranks and eventually dethroned Renan Barao to become the UFC bantamweight champion. Dillashaw's blend of striking and grappling skills, coupled with his relentless work ethic, has made him a formidable force in the division. His two victories over former champion Cody Garbrandt further cemented his legacy as one of the greatest bantamweights of all time.

3. Renan Barao

Renan Barao enjoyed an incredible reign as the UFC bantamweight champion, defending his title multiple times before losing it to TJ Dillashaw. Barao's explosive striking, slick submissions, and well-rounded skill set made him a force to be reckoned with. Although he faced some setbacks in his later career, Barao's dominance during his prime cannot be overlooked.

4. Urijah Faber

Urijah Faber, also known as "The California Kid," played a pivotal role in popularizing the bantamweight division and bringing attention to the lighter weight classes. Faber's charismatic personality, exciting fighting style, and numerous title fights have made him a fan favorite. While he fell short in his UFC title fights, Faber's impact on the division and his contributions to the sport cannot be denied.

5. Henry Cejudo

Henry Cejudo made history by becoming the first Olympic gold medalist in wrestling to win a UFC championship. After capturing the flyweight title, Cejudo moved up to the bantamweight division and defeated Marlon Moraes to claim the vacant belt. Cejudo's relentless pace, strong grappling skills, and underrated striking make him a formidable opponent for anyone in the division.

6. Cody Garbrandt

Cody Garbrandt burst onto the bantamweight scene with his impressive knockout power and quick hands. He became the UFC bantamweight champion by dethroning Dominick Cruz in a thrilling fight. Garbrandt's aggressive style and knockouts against top contenders have solidified his place among the top bantamweights of all time, despite recent setbacks.

7. Jose Aldo

Although primarily known for his dominance in the featherweight division, Jose Aldo also had a successful stint in the bantamweight division. Aldo's explosive striking, impeccable takedown defense, and overall skill set make him one of the greatest fighters in MMA history. While his time in the bantamweight division was relatively short-lived, Aldo's impact and skill cannot be ignored.

8. Marlon Moraes

Marlon Moraes, a former WSOF (World Series of Fighting) champion, made a successful transition to the UFC bantamweight division. Moraes' striking prowess, particularly his devastating leg kicks, has earned him numerous victories and highlight-reel knockouts. Although he fell short in his title fight against Henry Cejudo, Moraes remains one of the most dangerous fighters in the division.

9. Raphael Assuncao

Raphael Assuncao has been a consistent force in the bantamweight division for many years. With victories over notable fighters like TJ Dillashaw and Pedro Munhoz, Assuncao has proven his ability to compete at the highest level. While he may not have captured the UFC title, his technical skills and ability to adapt to different styles make him a respected contender.

10. Aljamain Sterling

Aljamain Sterling has steadily risen through the bantamweight ranks and has become one of the most well-rounded fighters in the division. Sterling's grappling skills, creative striking, and high fight IQ have propelled him to the top of the division. With his recent victory over Petr Yan to capture the UFC bantamweight championship, Sterling has solidified his place among the greatest bantamweights in UFC history.

The UFC bantamweight division has seen some of the most talented fighters in MMA history. From Dominick Cruz's elusive footwork to TJ Dillashaw's well-rounded skill set, these fighters have left an indelible mark on the sport. While opinions may differ on the rankings, the top 10 greatest UFC bantamweights of all time have undoubtedly entertained fans and pushed the boundaries of what is possible in the octagon.
