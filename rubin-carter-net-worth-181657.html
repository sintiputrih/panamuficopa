Rubin Carter Net Worth

Rubin Carter was a professional boxer born in Clifton, NJ on May 6, 1937. He was known as the Hurricane and had a career record of 27-12-1 before he was wrongfully convicted of murders he didn't commit and served nearly 20 years in prison. His story was later made into a movie, The Hurricane, starring Denzel Washington.
