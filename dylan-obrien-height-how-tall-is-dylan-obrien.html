Dylan OBrien height: How tall is Dylan OBrien?

Dylan Rhodes O’Brien who is popularly known as Dylan O’Brien was born on August 26, 1991 in New York City, United States of America to Patrick O’Brien, a camera operator and Lisa Rhodes, a former actress. He has a sister by name Julia O’Brien.

He is an American actor who had his first major role as Stiles Stilinski in Teen Wolf drama series from 2011 to 2017 and that was his breakthrough in his acting career.

O’Brien began his career little when he started producing, directing and acting short comedy films which he uploaded on his YouTube channel. He has starred in movies like High Road, The First Time, The internship, The Maze Runner among many other great movies.

Dylan O’Brien has gotten a lot of nominations and awards since 2013 to his credit.

How tall is Dylan O’Brien?

According to sources, Dylan O’Brien is 5 feet 10 inches or 177.8 cm tall. Dylan is of average height.
