Logan Storley Biography

Logan Storley is one of the most popular and richest Wrestler who was born on September 8, 1992 in Roslyn, South Dakota, United States.

Storley had one of the most accomplished high school wrestling careers in the history of his state, as he was a 5-time SDHSAA title holder and graduated from Webster High School with an unbelievable record of 262 wins and 3 losses throughout his 5 years of eligibility (competing in high school varsity) . He also competed in freestyle, winning the 2009 Fargo Nationals and the 2010 NHSCA Nationals as well.

At college he also accomplished multiple things while competing for the Golden Gophers. He became a 4-time All-American, placing 6th as a true freshman, 4th as a sophomore, 3rd as a junior and once again 4th as a senior at the NCAA Division I Championships. At the Big Ten Conference championships, he became the runner-up of the tournament as a freshman (falling to Ed Ruth), placed 7th as a sophomore and 4th as a junior and senior. He graduated in 2015 with a 119-27 record.
