Israeli couple save their ten-month-old twins by hiding them before they are killed while trying to


Israeli couple Itay and Hadar Berdichevsky, both 30, tragically lost their lives after managing to save their 10-month-old twin babies from the same fate. They reacted swiftly upon hearing gunmen attempting to break into their home. The terrified parents hurriedly hid their babies in a concealed shelter moments before the Hamas terrorists stormed into their residence. The couple bravely confronted the assailants but were ultimately shot and killed during the attack.

Following more than 12 hours of being left alone, the twins were found and rescued by Israeli soldiers. Israeli Ambassador to Colombia, Gali Dagan, praised the couple as heroes who did everything in their power to protect their children. Dagan highlighted the couple’s bravery in a tweet, stating, “They hid their 10-month-old twin children in the shelter while terrorists infiltrated their home. Itay and Hadar were brutally murdered after bravely fighting the terrorists.”

The tragic incident took place amid a surprise assault on Israel, resulting in the deaths of over 700 Israelis at the hands of Hamas terrorists. The couple’s heroic actions in the face of danger have left their children orphaned, leaving a lasting memory of their bravery.
