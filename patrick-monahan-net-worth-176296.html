Patrick Monahan Net Worth

Patrick Monahan is an American singer and songwriter best known as the lead singer and songwriter for the rock band Train. He was born on February 28, 1969 in Erie, PA and is most famous for his Grammy Award-winning song "Drops of Jupiter". Monahan has been a part of Train since its formation in 1994 and has released several successful albums with the band.
