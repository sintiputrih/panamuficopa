Beyond Wrestling Americanrana 23: Perfect Day Results (8/13): New IWTV Champion

On August 13, Beyond Wrestling presented their Americanrana ’23: Perfect Day event from the White Eagle in Worcester, Massachusetts.

In the main event, Alec Price defeated Matt Tremont to win the IWTV Independent Wrestling World Championship.

The results, courtesy of PWPonderings, are as follows:
