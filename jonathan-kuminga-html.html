Jonathan Kuminga - Age, Family, Bio

Congolese professional basketball player famous for playing for the Golden State Warriors. He was drafted by the Golden State Warriors with the 7th pick in the 2021 NBA Draft. He plays the positions small forward and power forward. 

Before Fame

He began playing basketball at the age of 2. By the time he was a junior in high school, several recruiting services listed him as number one in his class. 

Trivia

He posts selfies and basketball photos on his jonathan_kuminga Instagram account, which has amassed 330,000 followers. He has played on 3 teams throughout his professional career. 

Family Life

His brother, Joel Ntambwe, played basketball for Texas Tech University.

Associated With

His cousin Emmanuel Mudiay is a professional basketball player as well.
