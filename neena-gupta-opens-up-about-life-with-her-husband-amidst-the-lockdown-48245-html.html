Neena Gupta opens up about life with her husband amidst the lockdown

Actor Neena Gupta has said that she lived with her husband, Vivek Mehra for the first time as "husband and wife" during the COVID-19 lockdown last year. Both Gupta and Mehra had spent a large part of their lockdown at their holiday home in Mukteshwar. “My husband actually lives in Delhi and I live in Mumbai, that’s our base. So actually, for the first time, we lived as husband and wife here, in the lockdown. First time I came to know him and he came to know me," Gupta told a leading entertainment portal.

The Badhaai Ho actress first met Mehra on a flight from London to Mumbai. The couple who have known each other for over two decades, got married in 2008. “Initially, I used to crib, ‘Arre, we are together, but I hardly see you. You are always on the phone, I can’t talk to you.’ But now he says, ‘Arre, you are so busy! You are always on a call.’ And I am very happy like that, so I have learnt that I have to be busy myself. I read or do whatever. I cannot depend on him for my happiness or time to share. I call up my girlfriends and I talk to them. This first lockdown has changed a lot for me,” Gupta added.
