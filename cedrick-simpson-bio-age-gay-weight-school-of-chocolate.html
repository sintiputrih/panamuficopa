Cedrick Simpson Bio, Age, Gay, Weight Loss, IG

Attention food lovers! Netflix dropped its yummiest series ever School of Chocolate on November 26, 2021, and the casts are bursting with talent. But one contestant, Cedrick Simpson has specifically caught our eye, who has remarkably lost his weight and could potentially be gay.

So, what’s his story? Keep reading his Cedrick Simpson Bio to find out. 

Cedrick Simpson On School Of Chocolate

Netflix’s School of Chocolate was all about the eight pros including Cedrick Simpson who studied the art of chocolate under the tutelage of a famed chocolatier, Amaury Guichon. Throughout eight episodes, they learned tricks and skills that not only elevated their knowledge of pastry and chocolate but also gave them a life-changing experience.

But since this is a competition, only one will be named “Best in Class” and receive a $50,000 cash prize to start their own small business. Also, they’ll go on to each an international master class at Chef Amaury Guichon’s pastry academy in Las Vegas.

According to Cedrick, the toughest thing for him on the show was to break from his style to a completely new perspective.“Thinking outside of the box! Reflecting on it now, it all seemed so simple. I was able to trust the process and implement new techniques that were taught,” he added.

This season, Cedrick’s joined by co-contestants Juan Gutierrez, Amanda Miller, Daniel Joseph Corpuz, Mellisa Root, Stephanie Norcio, Thiago Silva, and Tyricia Clark. All equally talented. However, Cedrick might have a slight upper hand on a few others, as School of Chocolate wasn’t his first time on a reality show.

Previously, Cedrick participated in the sixth season of the Holiday Baking Championship that aired on Food Network in 2020. He was sent home over his sweet potato pie with cake mix crust, cranberry gelée, and brown sugar meringue. The judges said his meringue to be loose, the cranberries to be bland and the cake burnt.

His IMBD profile mentioned him to have appeared on 5 episodes of  Holiday Baking Championship, with the last being “Trendsetting Turkey Day.”

Is Cedrick Simpson Gay?

Cedrick Simpson didn’t officially reveal it but he could potentially be gay.

This School of Chocolate star often was spotted attending the pride parade and even uploaded an IG video from the one in September 2017. The post was captioned, “@chefbnay it’s almost that time 😂#pride #🌈 #Atl”

To which, @chefbnay replied, “Lmfaooo Last year was the best!!”

Earlier that year, Cedrick also made a post with a pride flag, hinting at his sexuality. “It’s soooooo pretty out today 🌞#spring #🌈,” the caption read.

Since Cedrick preferred privacy about his sexuality, he didn’t reveal any information about his dating life either.

As of 2021, Cedrick seemed single and was working at Capital City Club.

This pastry chef has previously been employed at The Whitley — A Luxury Collection Hotel, The Ritz-Carlton Hotel Company, LLC, and Earle Brown Heritage Center.

Cedrick Simpson Age

Cedrick Simpson was reportedly born before 1980. So, he couldn’t have been any younger than 41 years of age while he joined the School of Chocolate in 2021.

He was one of the older contestants who appeared on School of Chocolate.

Cedrick Simpson Weight Loss

Cedrick Simpson went through an amazing weight loss journey after 2019. Reportedly, he lost over 45 kg (100 lbs) just within a year.

The last we saw him, Cedrick posted an IG picture of his weight loss journey on November 22, 2021, captioned, “Another transformation.” And he looked chiseled this time.

Also, it was pretty noticeable that the pastry chef had been doing a lot more than just baking. We’re pretty sure he hit the guy at least once a week then.
