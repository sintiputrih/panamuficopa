Indian Canadian actor known for her work in Gujarati cinema. She made her film debut in 2016 with a role in the Telugu film Marala Telupana Priya. She later appeared in the Gujarati romantic comedy-drama Luv Ni Love Storys and the Hindi comedy horror film Bhool Bhulaiyaa 2.

She studied psychology at York University.
