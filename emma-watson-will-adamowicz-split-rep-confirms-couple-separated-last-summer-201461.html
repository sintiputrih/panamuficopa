Emma Watson, Will Adamowicz Split &quot;Last Summer,&quot; Rep Confirms to Us

It seems the love spell has faded for Emma Watson and her college flame Will Adamowicz. After rumors surfaced that the couple, who had been dating since late 2011, split, a rep confirmed to Us Weekly that the relationship had, in fact, ended.

"Emma and Will separated last summer and are no longer in touch," the rep told Us. 

The couple took their relationship public in April 2012, when they were spotted making out at the Coachella Music Festival in Indio, Calif. The fellow Brown University students originally met while studying abroad at the University of Oxford. Watson is currently a senior at Brown after leaving the school briefly to spend more time on her acting career.
