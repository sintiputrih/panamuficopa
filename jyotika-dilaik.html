Wiki/Biography

Jyotika Dilaik was born on Saturday, 23 July 1994 (age 27 years; as of 2021) in Shimla, Himachal Pradesh. Her zodiac sign is Leo. She did her schooling at Loreto Convent Tara Hall, Shimla, Himachal Pradesh.

Jyotika Dilaik’s childhood picture

She then attended Saint Bede’s College, Shimla, Himachal Pradesh. She pursued B.Tech. in Food Science and Technology from Shoolini University of Biotechnology and Management Sciences, Solan, Himachal Pradesh. [1] After completing her graduation, she started preparing herself for the civil services exams and attended a coaching institute in Chandigarh.

Family

Parents & Siblings

Her father, Gopal Dilaik, is a writer and has penned many Hindi books. Her mother’s name is Shakuntla Dilaik.

Jyotika Dilaik with her parents and fiance

She has two elder sisters. Her eldest sister, Rubina Dilaik, is a popular Indian TV actress, and she is married to the Indian TV actor Abhinav Shukla. Jyotika’s elder sister, Rohini Dilaik, is a blogger and resides in Canada.

Relationships/Affairs

While she was in college, she met Rajat Sharma who was her senior. After a few months, they were introduced to each other through a common friend. Later, they went on a trip with their common friends, and in the middle of their journey (through a train), Rajat proposed Jyotika, but she did not reply to it. In the final semester of college, while Jyotika went to South Korea on scholarship and Rajat was in Chandigarh, she realised her love for Rajat, and the couple started dating each other. After dating for a few years, the couple got engaged on 5 November 2021 in Shimla, Himachal Pradesh. Jyotika wore Rubina Dilaik’s lavender lehenga on her engagement day. [2]

Career

In 2020, amid the coronavirus lockdown, she returned to her home in Shimla and started a self-titled YouTube channel. She uploads daily vlogs on her YouTube channel, and as of December 2021, she has around 679K subscribers on her channel.
