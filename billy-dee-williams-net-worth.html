Billy Dee Williams Net Worth

Billy Dee Williams is an actor, musician, and TV pitchman who has a net worth of $9 million.

Billy Dee Williams is best known for his roles in films such as “The Empire Strikes Back,” “Return of the Jedi,” and “Batman.”

His long-time home was a magnificent Beverly Hills mansion he purchased in 1984 for $553,000. He offered the house for $4.5 million in 2011. He ultimately sold it for $2.8 million.

In addition to his film and television work, Billy Dee Williams has found success in television advertising, most notably in advertisements for Colt 45 malt liquor.

For his renowned portrayal as Lando Calrissian, Williams has also been a featured visitor at fan conventions, mainly science fiction ones. Williams returned to painting as his primary focus in the late 1980s.

Williams has released a jazz album titled “Let’s Misbehave” and written two books titled “PSI/Net” and “JUST/In Time.”
