Why Aunt Jane From The Five Nights At Freddys Movie Looks So Familiar

published on September 02, 2024
Mary Stuart Masterson’s breakout role in the 1987 film “Some Kind of Wonderful” not only showcased her talent but also challenged traditional gender roles. Originally written as a butch character named Keith, Masterson’s portrayal of Watts, a gender-neutral drummer, added depth and complexity to the story. Masterson’s dedication to the role, including learning to play the drums, further solidified her success.
