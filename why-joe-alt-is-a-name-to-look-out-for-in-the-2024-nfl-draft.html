Why Joe Alt is a Name to Look Out for in the 2024 NFL Draft

The 2024 NFL Draft is poised to welcome a plethora of talented prospects, and among them is Notre Dame's Joe Alt. With an impressive family legacy and a standout collegiate career, Alt has emerged as a top prospect for the upcoming draft. In this article, we will delve into the reasons why Joe Alt is a name to look out for in the 2024 NFL Draft.

Legacy and Background

Joe Alt hails from a family deeply rooted in football. His father, John Alt, was a first-round pick in the 1984 NFL Draft and enjoyed a successful 13-year career with the Kansas City Chiefs, earning Pro Bowl honors and establishing himself as a prominent figure in the league. Following in his father's footsteps, Joe Alt has positioned himself as a standout offensive tackle prospect, showcasing the potential to continue his family's legacy in the NFL.

Physical Attributes

Standing at an imposing 6'8" and weighing 315 pounds, Joe Alt possesses a towering frame with impressive length, setting the stage for his dominance on the field. His physical traits, including outstanding athleticism, recovery capacity, foot speed, agility, and flexibility, form a solid foundation for a prospect of his caliber.

Collegiate Success

Throughout his collegiate career at Notre Dame, Joe Alt has consistently demonstrated his prowess as an offensive tackle. Starting as a true freshman and excelling as a sophomore, he solidified his status as a force to be reckoned with on the left side of the offensive line. His achievements include being a first-team All-American and a dominating presence in the college football landscape.

Scouting Report

Strengths

  • Outstanding combo of size and athleticism
  • Zone scheme fit
  • Lateral agility
  • Nimble giant
  • Exceptional awareness and intelligence
  • Solid feet and quickness

Accomplishments

  • Joe Moore Award Semifinalist (2023)
  • Lombardi Award Semifinalist and Finalist (2023)
  • Associated Press All-American First Team (2022)
  • FWAA All-American Second Team (2022)
  • Shaun Alexander Freshman of the Year Watch List (2021)

Film Analysis

Joe Alt's performance on the field reflects his impressive athleticism, derived from his background in basketball and as a tight end. His agility, lower-body flexibility, and quickness are evident in his ability to effectively execute a range of offensive line techniques, making him a valuable asset for any team.

NFL Draft Prospects

Round 1 Potential

As the 2024 NFL Draft approaches, Joe Alt is garnering significant attention as a potential Round 1 pick. His physical attributes, collegiate accolades, and well-rounded skill set position him as a prime candidate for a franchise left tackle role in the NFL. With a high floor and a ceiling to match, Alt is seen as a day-one starting LT with Pro Bowl caliber potential.

Prospect Comparison

Alt has drawn comparisons to Mike McGlinchey, a highly regarded NFL offensive lineman. While Alt shares similarities with McGlinchey in terms of size and certain weaknesses, he is viewed as a safe pick to be a good NFL starter with Pro Bowl potential, further solidifying his standing as a top prospect in the 2024 NFL Draft.

Joe Alt emerges as a standout prospect in the 2024 NFL Draft, backed by a compelling combination of physical attributes, collegiate success, and a well-rounded skill set. With the potential to carry on his family's football legacy and make a significant impact at the professional level, Joe Alt is undoubtedly a name to look out for in the upcoming draft. As NFL teams prepare to make their selections, all eyes will be on Alt as he takes the next step in his football journey.

As the 2024 NFL Draft unfolds, the anticipation surrounding Joe Alt's future in the NFL is palpable, with many expecting him to make a significant impact at the professional level. With his exceptional talent and the potential to become a franchise left tackle, Alt is poised to be a prominent figure in the NFL for years to come.
