Jordan Beckham (Instagram Star) - Age, Family, Bio

Social star who has earned over 650,000 followers on her JordanBeckham_ Instagram account, where she is known to post a variety of fashion looks and also occasionally features her friends in her posts. 

Before Fame

She attended Oceanway Middle School in Jacksonville, Florida. 

Trivia

She has posted lip sync videos to popular rap, pop, and R&B songs to her Instagram account. She is a member of the TikTok collective Society Las Vegas. 

Family Life

She has a brother named Cole Beckham. She previously dated Garrison Hatley.

Associated With

She has posted lip syncs to songs by popular artists such as MadeinTYO and Future.
