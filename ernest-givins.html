Biography

Ernest Givins was born in Florida on September 3, 1964. Former 5’9″ NFL wide receiver who was named to two Pro Bowls and spent the bulk of his career with the Houston Oilers. He retired in 1995 with a total of 8,215 receiving yards and 49 touchdowns. He was a favorite target of quarterback Warren Moon on the Oilers. After graduating from Lakewood High School, he went on to play college football at Northeast Oklahoma Junior College before transferring to the University of Louisville.
