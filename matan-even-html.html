Matan Even - Age, Family, Bio

TikTok personality infamous for crashing events and shows. At the 2022 Game Awards, he invaded the stage and took the microphone to nominate his "reformed Orthodox rabbi Bill Clinton." He was carried off-stage and escorted away by the police. He has earned more than 1.2 million followers on his mataneven account.

Before Fame

He joined Twitter in March 2019.

Trivia

In October 2019, he gained international attention by waving a "Fight for Freedom, Stand up for Hong Kong" t-shirt while he was on television at the Los Angeles Clippers' home opening game. On Twitch, he often streams his opinions about American politics amongst the gameplay of Minecraft and Elden Ring. He has interviewed InfoWars' Owen Shroyer.

Family Life

He was born in Los Angeles. 

Associated With

He made a parody video, mocking Logan Paul's response to the CryptoZoo scandal.
