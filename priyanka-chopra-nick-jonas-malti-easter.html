Priyanka Chopra and Nick Jonas's Daughter Celebrates Easter in Adorable Onesie

Priyanka Chopra enjoyed a low-key, intimate Easter celebration with her baby daughter, Malti Marie, and even shared photos of the holiday festivities with fans on Instagram.

In the first snap, the Citadel star smiles for a selfie with one-year-old Malti, whose face is cut out of the picture. Malti wears an adorable white onesie with printed bunny ears and black lettering that reads, "Malti Marie's First Easter."

In a mirror selfie also included in the post, Chopra carries Malti in her arms and gives her a kiss on the top of her head. The mother-and-daughter duo are seen wearing a matching set, with Chopra in a T-shirt and Malti in a onesie. The mint-green set is covered all over with a delicate orange fruit-and-leaf pattern.

Other photos from the day show Malti attempting to munch on a chocolate Easter egg and, later, watching two dogs play in the grass while sitting on an outdoor lounge chair.
