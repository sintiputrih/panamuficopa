Leo Carlin dies at 86: Cause of death and obituary announcement

Leo Carlin has passed away. He was best known in both sports ticketing and the Philadelphia Eagles Hall of Fame. He will be remembered for his exceptional legacy innovative approaches to ticketing and deep connections within Eagles Nation communities that made him a legendary figure whose contributions will forever remain remembered by both.

Leo Carlin Cause of death

Leo Carlin’s cause of death was unrevealed at the time of his death announcement by the Philadelphia Eagles. He passed away peacefully on Wednesday, January 17, 2024, at the age of 86.

Leo Carlin’s obituary announcement

Leo Carlin became a member of the Philadelphia Eagles as a part-time ticketing department member in 1960, soon becoming a full-time front office in 1964 due to his dedication and talent. Over five decades, Carlin would remain an integral member of their organization – serving in 1971 at Veterans Stadium before making history by helping move them again to Lincoln Financial Field for 2003 playoff games.
